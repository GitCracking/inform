Internet Explorer: Pros and Cons

Internet Explorer is regarded as one of the worst browsers to ever exist. With a reputation for being slow and vulnerable, Internet Explorer has slowly faded away. Yet, many people still use Internet Explorer.

What are Internet Explorer's stats?

  • Internet Explorer has had multiple security vulnerabilites, with major ones in 2006, 2008, and 2010
  • Has a restricted mode for the browser that helps limit the spread from malware
  • The UK and Germany both warned against using Internet Explorer at times
  • As of April 2018, Internet Explorer had a 3.2% usage share of all browsers
  • Has many policies that makes it useful for companies
  • Is tied with Chrome at loading multiple tabs at once

While Internet Explorer has seen a resurgence in recent years, experts advise against using it.
